## 2024 Key Changes
- Paladin & Ranger: Spellcasting at level 1 (was 2)
- All classes: Epic Boon at level 19
- Weapon Mastery: Barbarian, Fighter, Paladin, Ranger, Rogue at level 1
- Monk: Ki → Discipline Points, martial arts die scales higher
- Sorcerer: Prepares spells (was known), more available
- Warlock: Invocations at level 1, Pact Boon merged into invocations
- Fighter: Up to 4 Extra Attacks at level 20 (was 3)
- Divine Smite is now a spell (can be Counterspelled)
